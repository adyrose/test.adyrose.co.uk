The gigafactory

An $800m investment across two phases

Anglo-Korean next-generation battery company Eurocell EMEA is set to build its first European Gigafactory producing proven, production-ready technologies by 2023 – far faster than many rivals.

As the final phase of a 15-year, $100m R&D programme, the Eurocell Gigafactory will supply Europe’s energy storage, automotive, e-mobility and consumer electronics industries.

The chosen Gigafactory location will create hundreds of direct jobs and many more indirect employment opportunities. It carries with it a strong ESG focus and a comprehensive knowledge transfer programme bringing vital skills from Korean battery experts and boosting the economy in a strategically crucial sector.
